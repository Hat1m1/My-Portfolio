Hi! My name is Hatim.

Nearly 4 years ago, I moved to the United Kingdom from Spain, not knowing that this decision would impact my entire life for many reasons. The first challenge was not having any friends, and I could not speak a word of English. I also had to navigate a completely new education system that I had never been introduced to before. On top of that, I had only one year to prepare for my GCSEs, which at the time, I did not even know existed. The most difficult part about this was that I had no one to support me through this journey.

I believe that most people in my position would have simply given up, as there were many obstacles to overcome. However, not me, I never considered giving up as an option. To improve my English, I used movies, videos, and books. I even went outside to parks to talk with people, regardless of their age, just to practice speaking.

Believe it or not, after overcoming the initial challenges of making friends, learning English, and familiarising myself with life in the UK, I had only 38 days left to revise for my GCSEs! Again, giving up was never an option, so I spent 5 hours studying after every school day and 8 hours on weekends. After all these efforts, I managed to achieve amazing results, averaging a grade 7 across all 9 GCSE subjects I chose.
